How To Play

  • Choose a difficulty to begin the 30 seconds timer.
  • Select a card to uncover the number, select another card to try and match a pair.
  • Matching pairs will remain uncovered, incorrrect pairs will be hidden again.
  • Every attempt at a pair adds 1 to your score.
  • Every second adds 1 to your score.
  • The game ends when your time runs out or when all pairs are found.
  • The lower the score, the better. 50 or less on Hard mode unlocks Extreme mode.
